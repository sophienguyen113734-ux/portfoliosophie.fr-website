It had taught me to put myself in another person's shoes — and clearly, it made me realize how lucky I was. Exhibitor School fair

School fair exhibitor, discovering that leadership lives in presence and listening, not volume.

The smell of fresh-printed flyers and the echoing sound of people talking always reminds me of the day I went to represent my school as an exhibitor at a School Fair.
